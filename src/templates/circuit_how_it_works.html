How Circuit Works

We use behavioral science to help you build genuine connections, not endless engagement.

1. Voice-First Profiles

Skip the filtered photos and curated bios. Record a short voice intro that captures your personality authentically.

2. Smart Matching

Our algorithm analyzes communication patterns, values, and interests to find compatible people in your community.

3. Community Events

Join local events and activities based on shared interests. Meet people in real life, not through endless messaging.

4. No Dopamine Traps

No infinite scroll. No addictive notifications. We measure success by meaningful connections, not screen time.

The Anti-Addiction Approach

Most social apps exploit psychological vulnerabilities to maximize engagement. Circuit is built on opposite principles:

  • Finite interactions: We limit daily matches to encourage thoughtful engagement over endless swiping.
  • Quality over quantity: Our algorithm prioritizes compatibility depth over surface-level attraction.
  • Real-world focus: We push you toward in-person meetings rather than keeping you trapped in-app.
  • Transparent design: No dark patterns. No hidden engagement tricks. Just honest connection tools.
