Frequently Asked Questions

Circuit is designed to help you build genuine connections, not maximize engagement. We use voice-first profiles, limit daily matches, and measure success by users who leave because they've found their community.

Instead of swiping on photos, you record a short voice intro. This lets potential matches hear your actual personality before connecting - more authentic than curated photos.

Circuit's core features are free. We offer optional premium features, but connecting with compatible people costs nothing.

We collect minimal data and never sell your information. You control what appears on your profile. Voice recordings are encrypted and can be deleted anytime.

Use the in-app block and report features. Blocked users cannot contact you again. Our team reviews reports within 24 hours.
