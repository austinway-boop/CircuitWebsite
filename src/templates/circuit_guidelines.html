Community Guidelines

The standards we all uphold to make Circuit a place for genuine connection.

Be Authentic

  • Use your real identity: No impersonation or misleading info
  • One account per person: Multiple accounts are prohibited
  • Honest communication: Don't misrepresent your intentions

Be Respectful

  • No harassment: Unwanted contact or intimidation is not tolerated
  • No hate speech: Discrimination of any kind is prohibited
  • Respect boundaries: Accept "no" gracefully
  • No unsolicited explicit content

Be Safe

  • No illegal activity
  • No commercial activity: No solicitation or advertising
  • Protect minors: Circuit is for adults 18+
  • No scams: Financial solicitation results in permanent bans

Enforcement

First violation (minor): Warning

Repeated violations: Temporary suspension

Serious violations: Permanent ban

Illegal activity: Immediate ban + report to authorities
