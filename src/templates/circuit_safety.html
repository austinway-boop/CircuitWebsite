Safety at Circuit

Your safety is our priority. Here's how we protect our community.

Identity Verification

All users go through verification. No bots, no catfish, no fake accounts.

Voice Authentication

Voice intros confirm real humans. We detect AI-generated audio.

24/7 Moderation

Trained moderators review reports around the clock.

Privacy by Design

Minimal data collection. Your info is never sold.

Safety Features

Before Meeting

  • Profile verification badges
  • Voice-only chat before sharing photos
  • Mutual interest matching required

During Interactions

  • Easy one-tap blocking
  • Detailed report system
  • AI-powered harassment detection

Reporting Concerns

If you experience harassment or feel unsafe:

  • Use the in-app report button
  • Email safety@circuit.app
  • For emergencies, contact local authorities
