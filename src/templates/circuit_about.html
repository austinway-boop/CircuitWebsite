About Circuit

Social matchmaking that solves gaming's retention problem.

The Core Insight

Current matchmaking optimizes for skill balance and queue times. But the brain distinguishes between reward-based dopamine and social dopamine.

You build tolerance to reward dopamine in 2-3 weeks. Social dopamine? 6-8 weeks. Players don't quit because they lose interest - they quit because reward loops burn out.

Social loops scale. We're building the infrastructure to make that happen.

Perfectly balanced matches create forgettable relationships. Real friendship forms through shared struggle.

The best matchmaking isn't fair - it's intentional. When success depends on others, strangers become allies.
